ABOUT


Azamat Abdullaev and Laziz Abdullaev a.k.a Dropfire are Uzbekistan based music producers. As they have been putting their hands in production till the present, Dropfire has already gained a 5-year-experience. This has been a super efficient period for them, inasmuch as they have shown how big they are with myriad super exciting masterpieces, which appealed to many big names worldwide.

Collaboration Offer


Dropfire is always open for collaboration offers. However, the only request is you should attach a collab idea at the same message You will not recieve a reply to empty collab offers (without a demo) in any way!

Remix Request


You can ask for an official remix for your original song, if and only if your track meets our standarts. Contact Dropfire for more info (price etc.)
Contact at: dropfiremusic@gmail.com
